About Our Firm

Kooben Chetty & Associates is a distinguished legal firm with a rich history spanning over 39 years. Our specialization spans various facets of law, supported by a committed team devoted to delivering optimal client service. This commitment is underscored by our core values of expertise, compassion, integrity, and a commitment to fostering a positive client experience. These values collectively constitute an integral aspect of our organizational culture.
